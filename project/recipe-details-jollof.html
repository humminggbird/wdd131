Jollof Rice

A smoky, spicy West African rice dish cooked in a rich tomato-pepper base, often served with chicken or beef.

Jollof Ingredients

  • • 3 cups rice
  • • 1 cup blended tomatoes
  • • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • • 1 onion
  • • Scotch bonnet pepper
  • • Stock cubes
  • • Chicken or beef (optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, pepper, and onions.
  2. Fry mixture until thick and aromatic.
  3. Add tomato paste and spices.
  4. Add rice and stock.
  5. Cook on low heat until rice absorbs sauce.

Helpful Cooking Tips

💡 Smoky flavor

Let rice slightly burn at the bottom for party-style aroma.

💡 Stock matters

Use meat stock instead of water for richer taste.
