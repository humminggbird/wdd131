Fufu and Light Soup

Soft pounded fufu served with a spicy tomato-based light soup made with meat or fish.

🔥
650Calories
🥩
30gProtein
⏱️
70 minsPrep Time
🔪
HardDifficulty
Recipe added to your meal plan!

Ingredients

  • • Cassava
  • • Plantain
  • • Tomatoes
  • • Onion
  • • Meat or fish
  • • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil cassava and plantain.
  2. Pound into smooth fufu.
  3. Cook meat or fish stock.
  4. Add blended tomato mixture.
  5. Simmer and serve hot.

Nutrition Information

Calories 650
Protein 30g
Carbohydrates 80g
Fat 22g
Fiber 6g

Tips

💡 Pound fast

Pound fufu while hot for smooth texture.

💡 Smoked fish

Smoked fish adds deep aroma to soup.
